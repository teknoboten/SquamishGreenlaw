Who is Lauren? (she/her)

Hi, I'm Lauren! A mother, geologist, and environmentalist, living in Squamish with my partner, some small humans and our dog, Butte. I'm running for council because I want to be a part of making long-term decisions in the best interest of the land and people of our city.

For the last 20 years I have dedicated my life to studying the earth. I'm knowledgeable about the environment and am genuinely concerned about the future. I believe that equality is essential for our future. Environmental degradation and inequality are deeply intertwined; the intersection is my passion and main motivator. I hope to have the opportunity to use this passion to serve our community here in Squamish.
